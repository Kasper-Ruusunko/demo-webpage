Gaming Laptop Pro

€899.99

The Gaming Laptop Pro is designed for serious gamers and professionals who demand exceptional performance. Featuring a powerful Intel Core i7 processor and NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4060 graphics card, this laptop delivers smooth gameplay and excellent productivity performance.

With 16GB of DDR5 RAM and a 1TB NVMe SSD, you'll have plenty of memory and storage for all your games, applications, and files. The 15.6-inch Full HD display with 144Hz refresh rate ensures crisp visuals and fluid motion for competitive gaming.

Key features include advanced cooling system, RGB backlit keyboard, Wi-Fi 6 connectivity, and a long-lasting battery. Whether you're gaming, streaming, or working on demanding projects, this laptop has the power to handle it all.
